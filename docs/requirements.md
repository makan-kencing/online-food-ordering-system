Mandatory Module:
1. Member registration (free of charge for “Normal Member” and RM6.00 per
month for “VIP Member”).
2. Allow members to order food or drink items online from any available
restaurants.
3. Manage the registration of the restaurant and its food or drink items.
4. Enable advanced search options for restaurants (e.g., search by name, category:
Halal or Non-Halal, rating, location and others) and food or drink items (e.g.,
search by price, type, budget meal, super deal and others).
5. Member can opt to pickup the food or drink items or request delivery service
from any food delivery company (e.g. Grab Food, Food Panda or others with
delivery charge).
6. Manage redemption of discount and/or free delivery vouchers from members.
Additional Modules (Each team is required to choose a different module):
7. Member Feedback Management: manage feedback or complaint from members.
